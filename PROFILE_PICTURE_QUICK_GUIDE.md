# 📸 Profile Picture Upload - Quick Guide

## How to Use

### Step 1: Open Edit Profile
1. Go to **Profile** screen (bottom navigation)
2. Click **"Edit Profile"** button

### Step 2: Choose Upload Method

You'll see your current profile picture with two buttons below:

#### Option A: Take Photo 📸
- Click **"Take Photo"** button
- Allow camera permission when prompted
- See yourself in the camera preview
- Click **"Capture"** to take the photo
- Click **"Cancel"** if you want to try again

#### Option B: Choose from Gallery 🖼️
- Click **"Choose from Gallery"** button
- Select an image from your device
- Image must be less than 5MB
- Preview updates automatically

### Step 3: Save Changes
- Review your new profile picture in the preview
- Click **"Save Changes"** button
- Done! Your profile picture is updated ✨

## Tips

### For Best Results:
1. **Good Lighting**: Take photos in well-lit areas
2. **Center Your Face**: Position yourself in the center
3. **Clean Background**: Use a simple background
4. **Smile**: Show your personality! 😊

### Troubleshooting:
- **Camera not working?** → Allow camera permission in browser
- **Image too large?** → Choose a smaller image (max 5MB)
- **Wrong file type?** → Select an image file (JPG, PNG, etc.)
- **Preview not updating?** → Try refreshing the page

## Need Help?

### Common Questions:

**Q: Can I remove my profile picture?**
A: Yes, just upload a new one or leave it as default.

**Q: What image formats are supported?**
A: JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, and most common image formats.

**Q: Is there a size limit?**
A: Yes, maximum 5MB per image.

**Q: Can I crop the image?**
A: Currently no, but you can crop before uploading.

**Q: Does it work on mobile?**
A: Yes! Works on iOS and Android devices.

**Q: What if I deny camera permission?**
A: The app will automatically open the gallery picker instead.
