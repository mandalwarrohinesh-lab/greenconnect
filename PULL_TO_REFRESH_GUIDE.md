# рҹ“ұ Pull-to-Refresh - Simple Guide

## How to Refresh the App

### Pull Down to Refresh рҹ‘Ү

**Steps:**
1. Make sure you're at the **top of the screen**
2. **Pull down** with your finger
3. Keep pulling until you see **"Release to refresh"**
4. **Release** your finger
5. Watch the refresh animation
6. Done! Content is updated вңЁ

## Tips

### вң… Do:
- Pull from the **top of the screen**
- Pull **at least 80px** (about 1 inch)
- Wait for **"Release to refresh"** text
- Release your finger to trigger refresh

### вқҢ Don't:
- Pull from middle of screen (won't work)
- Pull too little (needs 80px minimum)
- Pull while scrolling (wait until at top)

---

## Troubleshooting

### Not Working?
1. вң… Scroll to the **very top** of the screen
2. вң… Pull down **slowly** and deliberately
3. вң… Pull **far enough** (about 1 inch)
4. вң… Check your **internet connection**

### Still Not Working?
- Refresh the page (browser refresh)
- Check if you're at the top of the screen
- Try pulling slower
- Make sure touch is enabled
